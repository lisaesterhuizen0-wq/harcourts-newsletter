The Market This Week

South Africa's residential property market continues to demonstrate encouraging resilience heading into the second quarter of 2026, with buyer activity holding steady across the mid to upper price bands as improved consumer confidence follows the cumulative effect of interest rate relief seen through 2025 and early 2026. First time buyers remain particularly active, spurred on by more accessible bond qualification thresholds and a growing sense that now represents a genuine window of opportunity before values climb further. Sellers in well located suburbs are finding that correctly priced stock moves quickly, with multiple offer scenarios becoming increasingly common in sought after nodes, signalling that demand is meaningfully outpacing quality supply in key areas.
