The Market This Week

South Africa's residential property market has entered a notably more active phase in the first quarter of 2026, with buyer confidence recovering steadily following a series of interest rate cuts that began in the second half of 2024 and continued into early 2025. First-time buyers, who had been sitting on the sidelines during the high-rate environment of 2023 and 2024, are now returning to the market in meaningful numbers, particularly in the sub-R2.5 million price band, where bond approvals have improved substantially.
